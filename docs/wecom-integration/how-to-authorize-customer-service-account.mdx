---
title: Authorizing Customer Service Account in Wecom Admin Console
sidebar_position: 6
description: Now we have authorized the Customer Service Accounts for the Third Party application in Wecom.
---
1.  Login to the Wecom Admin Console. App Management → Apps → WeChat Customer Service Account

![Illustration](/img/inline-images/wecom-integration/how-to-authorize-customer-service-account/1758603090250.png)

2.  Inside the WeChat Customer Service Account - We can see the list of Customer Service Accounts in the Wecom Account.

![Illustration](/img/inline-images/wecom-integration/how-to-authorize-customer-service-account/1758603090268.png)

3.  Go to the Last part of the page and Click on the Authorize third Party apps.

![Illustration](/img/inline-images/wecom-integration/how-to-authorize-customer-service-account/1758603090266.png)

4.  You can see the list of Third Party applications that are already authorized in Wecom. Click on the three dots on the desired third party application (In the below illustration it is shown as IMTest1 But our in live it should be selected as ZohoIM) and click on the Authorize Customer Service Account.

![Illustration](/img/inline-images/wecom-integration/how-to-authorize-customer-service-account/1758603090325.png)

5.  It will list out the Customer Service Accounts in wecom Application. Please select the customer Service Account that are desired to be authorized.

![Illustration](/img/inline-images/wecom-integration/how-to-authorize-customer-service-account/1758603090288.png)

Now we have authorized the Customer Service Accounts for the Third Party application in Wecom.

6.  It will show like below after authorization.

![Illustration](/img/inline-images/wecom-integration/how-to-authorize-customer-service-account/1758603090141.png)
