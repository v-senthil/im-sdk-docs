## 1\. Messaging Restrictions

-   Proactive messaging is not allowed: Unlike WhatsApp, we cannot initiate conversations. The customer must first reach out to the Customer Service Account before communication can begin.
-   Reply window: Agents can reply within 48 hours of the customer’s last message. Beyond this window, responses are not permitted.
-   Message limit: An agent can send a maximum of 5 messages after the customer’s last message.

## 3\. Layout Messages

Layout messages are supported with three options only:

1.  Text
2.  Click – Clickable Button Reply Menu
3.  View – URL Button

⚠️ Note: Attachments are not supported in Layout Messages.
